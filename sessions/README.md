# Session Logs

Keep records of play sessions here if you want to track what happened.

## Why Keep Logs?

- Remember what happened when you pick up after a break
- Track decisions and their consequences
- Build up a history that makes the world feel lived-in
- Reference for continuity

## Template

```markdown
# Session [Number] - [Date]

## Summary
[2-3 sentence overview of what happened]

## Key Events
- [Event 1]
- [Event 2]
- [Event 3]

## Decisions Made
- [Decision and reasoning]

## NPCs Encountered
- [Name - who they are, how it went]

## Open Threads
- [Things left unresolved to pick up later]

## Notes
[Anything else worth remembering]
```

## Example

```markdown
# Session 1 - 2026-01-20

## Summary
The Pathfinder departed from the Ganymede gate, beginning its journey toward the Signal. First day of FTL travel was uneventful, but crew tensions emerged between the corporate liaison and the science team.
```
