# AtmoTech Industries

**Headquarters:** Europa Station, Luna  
**Primary Business:** Terraforming technology, atmospheric processing, environmental engineering  
**Revenue:** $420 billion annually  
**Employees:** 890,000

Pan-European company, founded 2098. Dominates terraforming and atmospheric science. Mars terraforming relies on AtmoTech systems. Venus operations largely AtmoTech technology.

**Power:** Controls essential technology for humanity's expansion. Mars literally cannot continue terraforming without AtmoTech expertise and equipment.

**Reputation:** European precision and environmental consciousness—genuinely tries to balance profit with ecological responsibility. Still a megacorp, but less ruthless than competitors.

# Vanguard Mining Group

**Headquarters:** Ceres  
**Primary Business:** Asteroid mining, rare earths and platinum group metals  
**Revenue:** $380 billion annually  
**Employees:** 950,000

Western Alliance-registered, founded by Belt independents who sold out (or sold up) in 2140s. Known for treating workers better than competitors.

**Power:** Second-largest Belt miner, technical expertise, good relationship with independent prospectors.

**Reputation:** The "good" megacorp—relatively speaking. Better pay, better conditions, actual safety standards. Still extracts maximum profit, just does it with more human face.

# SolBank Consortium

**Headquarters:** Shea Crater, Luna  
**Primary Business:** Banking, finance, investment, insurance  
**Revenue:** $290 billion annually  
**Employees:** 450,000

Not a single bank but a consortium of Earth's largest financial institutions operating in space. Controls system-wide financial infrastructure.

**Power:** Money itself—controls capital flows, can make or break ventures through credit access.

**Reputation:** Bloodless, calculating, politically neutral (serves all blocs equally). Universally necessary, universally resented.

---

# BioFrontier Corporation

**Headquarters:** Lagos, Earth; operations on Mars and Luna  
**Primary Business:** Biotech, agricultural systems, medical technology  
**Revenue:** $310 billion annually  
**Employees:** 680,000

African Union-originated, expanded globally. Now developing Mars-adapted agriculture and advanced medical treatments for space environments.

**Power:** Controls essential food production technology, cutting-edge medical tech.

**Reputation:** Genuine commitment to improving human welfare alongside profit. Youngest major megacorp, still hungry and ambitious.
