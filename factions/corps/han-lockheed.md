# Han-Lockheed Orbital

**Headquarters:** New Armstrong, Luna (nominal); operations across system  
**Primary Business:** Spacecraft construction, advanced propulsion  
**Revenue:** $890 billion annually  
**Employees:** 2.4 million

---

## Overview

Formed from merger of Chinese Han Aerospace and American Lockheed Martin in 2145, Han-Lockheed dominates high-end spacecraft construction. If you need an advanced vessel, they're likely involved.

---

## Power

- Controls the Galilean Shipyards (between Ganymede and Callisto)
- Operates New Shanghai on Titan (their corporate fiefdom)
- Builds most Federal and corporate spacecraft
- Federal Government needs them more than they need Federal Government

---

## Reputation

Ruthlessly efficient, politically connected, willing to bend rules but smart enough not to break them obviously.

## Saturn Operations

New Shanghai on Titan is nominally a hydrocarbon mining operation. In reality:
- Corporate fiefdom with no real Federal oversight
- Money laundering hub
- Black market nexus
- Refuge for those fleeing Federal jurisdiction

The Federal Government pretends it's legitimate. Han-Lockheed pretends to follow rules. Everyone knows the truth.

---

## Relationship with Federal Government

Complicated. Han-Lockheed:
- Builds Federal ships (including components of the *Prometheus*)
- Resents Federal FTL monopoly
- Lobbies constantly for "technology sharing"
- Almost certainly engaged in espionage

The Federal Government:
- Needs Han-Lockheed's manufacturing capacity
- Suspects their espionage activities
- Can't afford to confront them directly
- Watches them very carefully
