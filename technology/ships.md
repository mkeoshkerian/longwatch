# Ships and Propulsion

## Conventional Propulsion

For non-FTL travel, humanity uses fusion drives as standard:

### Fusion Torch Drives
- Controlled fusion reaction directing plasma exhaust
- Enables 0.1-0.3g constant acceleration
- Inner system trips measured in weeks
- Jupiter run takes 2-3 months

### Ion Drives
- For cargo and efficient long-haul
- Slow but fuel-efficient

### Chemical Rockets
- Still used for surface-to-orbit on Earth and Mars
- Mostly replaced by orbital elevators on Luna

| Fleet Destroyer | 200m | 80 | Screen capital ships |

### Longwatch

| Class | FTL | Vessels | Notes |
|-------|-----|---------|-------|
| Prometheus-class | YES | 1 (+ 1 under construction) | Only FTL-capable ships |
| Explorer-class | No | 11 | Deep space exploration, no FTL |

### Civilian/Corporate

- **Freighters:** Bulk cargo between major ports
- **Mining vessels:** Belt and Jupiter operations
- **Passenger liners:** Earth-Mars-Belt routes
- **Corporate security:** Varies widely in capability
